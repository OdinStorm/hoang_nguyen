INTRODUCTION

Hoang Nguyen Vung Tau Co., Ltd. would like to send most sincere greeting to our valued customers.

Our company Hoang Nguyen Vung Tau (HNVT) is specialized in marine services and has its business area in Vung Tau City and Ho Chi Minh City, the two most dynamic and developed areas of maritime field in Viet Nam. In the International economic integration, with our strong internal capability and close cordination with professional partners in shipping industry, HNVT has been providing a variety of qualified services for many large offshore projects, adequiately met the needs of domestic and international customers.

Based on experience and reputation gained through many jobs that we have carried out, HNVT always wants to provide customers with services of best quality and most competitive price with the slogan Smooth Sailing.

HNVT would be grateful to receive attention and trust from our valued customers for our qualified marine services.
